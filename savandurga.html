About This Trek

Savandurga is one of the largest monoliths in Asia, located about 45 km from Bangalore near Magadi town. The twin hills — Karigudda (black hill) and Biligudda (white hill) — offer stunning panoramic views of the Arkavathi river and surrounding countryside. It's an ideal day trek for beginners and is especially popular for early morning sunrise hikes. The trail takes about 3–4 hours round trip and is well marked throughout.
